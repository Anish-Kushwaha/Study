Creator Information

FocusRoom is independently designed and developed by Anish Kushwaha as a privacy-first, distraction-free study platform dedicated to CISCE (ICSE & ISC) students preparing for the 2026 board examinations.

This platform is built with the philosophy that technology should support focus, not exploit attention.

Privacy Policy

FocusRoom respects your privacy by design. This website operates entirely on the client side and does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on any external servers.

  • No user accounts or logins are required.
  • No tracking cookies, analytics, or third-party trackers are used.
  • No personal information is collected or shared.
  • All data (timers, goals, notes) is stored locally in your browser.
  • Clearing your browser data permanently removes all stored information.

FocusRoom does not use advertising networks, fingerprinting, screen monitoring, or behavioral analytics.
