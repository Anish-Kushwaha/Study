Privacy Policy

FocusRoom is built with privacy-first principles:

  • No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or log any personal information
  • No Tracking: No cookies, analytics, or user behavior tracking
  • Local Storage Only: Study rooms and data exist only in your browser's localStorage
  • No Backend Servers: Everything runs locally on your device
  • Automatic Cleanup: Rooms automatically delete after 24 hours of inactivity
